Jessica and Julia's Favorite Places

Iceland

Camper Van

Driving around the country was amazing!

Get ready for hotdogs in the back of a car. They are delicous and economical.

And accidental offroading adventures when you follow a GPS.

We got our van at Camp Easy!

Waterfalls

They are everywhere! We saw at least one a day, some just on the side of the road.

We helped a couple take a gender reveal photo in front of one! Everyone got very wet!

Some of my favorites:

  • Skogafoss
  • Dettifoss
  • Selfoss
  • Godafoss

Maui

Beaches

Warm water and gentle surf on the South Shore means great snorkeling, paddleboarding, and more!

Visit a clothing-optional beach on your way to the airport and you won't have to pack a wet swimsuit!

Activities

Maui is the place to try it all!

  • Skin diving with turtles
  • Zodiac raft whale-watching with baby humpbacks
  • Do your open-water SCUBA cert in the best place possible!
  • Stand-up paddle surfing or traditional surfing
  • lounge by the pool
  • Tips for Driving the West Maui Loop:
    1. Proceed with EXTREME CAUTION!
    2. Do not travel after dark!
    3. Make sure that your passenger has good stress-management skills.
